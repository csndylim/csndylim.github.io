About

Temasek Polytechnic ➼ Nanyang Technological University

"One plus one is equals to ten, and not two."

The statement puzzled and questioned me on the logic of my strongest subject in Secondary School, Mathematics. I was a participant at Nanyang Polytechnic’s School of IT Day Camp for ‘O’ level students. It turned out that the statement was solved using binary arithmetic instead of decimal arithmetic. That concept sparked my interest in technology and led me to choose Diploma in Big Data Management & Governance at Temasek Polytechnic. My polytechnic had provided me with many enriching opportunities in the field of Data Analytics but there is a need to learn beyond the classroom to gain exposure to the latest technologies. During my free time, I would attend many tech exhibitions and workshops from Meetup.com which are organised by the local tech communities. These events had given me exposure on real-life use cases and augmented my knowledge and skills. However, I felt that I am lacking on the fundamentals of Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, DevOps, Fintech, IoT, and Web Design etc. Thus, I have decided to pursue an undergraduate degree in Computer Science at Nanyang Technological University, and concurrently pursue a one-year Specialist Diploma in Cloud Data Centre Technology & Management at Temasek Polytechnic. This exciting adventure can help me to broaden my knowledge and skills, and at the same time, to develop my Computer Science portfolio.

This is the reason on why I will still stay in the technology domain.
